## How to Edit the Templates  

### **For Quick Edits (Using the Provided PNG/SVG Files)**  
- Open your preferred **design software** (e.g., Inkscape, Photoshop, or Figma).  
- Import the empty **PNG/SVG** template.  
- Make adjustments as needed.  

### **For Full Customization (Using Canva)**  
1. **Access the Canva Templates:**  
   - Visit the shared [Canva Template Link](https://www.canva.com/design/DAGb-cBfGfw/bqOYWbJZhOMXuuDycxRP0A/edit?utm_content=DAGb-cBfGfw&utm_campaign=designshare&utm_medium=link2&utm_source=sharebutton).  
   - Ensure you are logged into your **Canva account**.  

2. **Duplicate the Template:**  
   - Click on **"File" > "Make a copy"** to create your own editable version.  
   - Rename the copy for organization.  

3. **Customize the Design:**  
   - Update **text or images 
   - Maintain the **font styles** and **layout** for consistency.  

4. **Download & Share:**  
   - Export the final design in **PNG/JPG** and share.
